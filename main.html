How to Use



1. Utensil Detergent


1. Fill a sink or dishwashing basin with warm water
2. Add the recommended amount of detergent to the water
3. Place the dirty utensils into the water and let them soak for a few minutes
3. Scrub the utensils with a scrub brush or sponge to remove any stubborn food residue or stains
4. Rinse the utensils thoroughly with clean water
5. Dry the utensils with a clean cloth or let them air dry.


2. Toilet Detergent


1. Pour the desired amount of detergent into the toilet bowl or spread it on the surface with a brush.
2. Scrub the inside of the bowl and the outside surfaces of the toilet with a brush or scrubber.
3. Let the detergent sit for a few minutes, as directed on the product label, to allow it to work on stains and dirt.
4. Flush the toilet to rinse away the detergent and debris.
5. Repeat the process, if necessary, until the toilet is clean and sanitized.
6. Remember to use gloves and follow all safety precautions listed on the product label when using toilet detergents.


3. Bathroom Detergent


1. Wet the surface you want to clean.
2. Apply a small amount of bathroom detergent to the surface.
3. Scrub the surface with a cloth or brush to remove dirt, soap scum, and other debris.
4. Rinse the surface thoroughly with water.
5. Wipe the surface dry with a clean cloth, exercise caution when using bleach or other cleaning agents to avoid damage to surfaces or skin.
